# Pot Roast

## Ingredient

* roast
* bottle wine
* 1 onion
* celery stalks
* carrots
* oil
* salt & pepper
* garlin
* 1 Tbsp flour
* 1/2 tsp sugar
* thyme
* rosemary

bring to simmer, braise at 300 for 3 hours, tent meat

add thyme and bring broth to simmer, reduce to 3 1/2 cups. strain
out solids for about 1 1/2 cups of sauce.
